Best Parts of a Program

  1. Copious* amounts of Syntax.
  2. Comments.
  3. Simple code.
  4. Line spacing.
  5. No unwarranted* code.
  6. Program makes sense.
  7. Does not need a special program to run.
  8. Self dependent (no refrences to a certain computer)
  9. Product looks clean.
  10. Works on the introduction*, and every time after that.
